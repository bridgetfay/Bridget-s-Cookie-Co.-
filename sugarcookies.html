Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the shortening and 1 1/2 cups sugar.
  3. Stir in the eggs.
  4. Sift together the flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt
  5. Stir into the creamed mixture until well blended.
  6. In a small bowl, stir together the 2 tablespoons of sugar, and the cinnamon.
  7. Roll dough into walnut sized balls, then roll the balls in the cinnamon-sugar.
  8. Place them onto an unprepared cookie sheet, two inches apart.
  9. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes once oven is preheated
  10. Edges should be slightly brown.
  11. Remove from sheets to cool on wire racks.
  12. ENJOY :)
